Header

Sketch Meetup Stuttgart

I entered a design contest for the Sketch Meetup Stuttgart in July 2018. The task was to design a header that represents Sketch and Stuttgart. This is the piece I handed in.

The Flat Design gives the header a clean and bright look. The buildings represent where the Meetup is located. Lastly, the clouds emphasis the abstract thinking that is needed to create designs.

Logo

Mint-Up

In May 2017, I designed the logo for the agency Mint-Up. The goal was a design that reflects the philosophy of the agency.

The color green represents the freshness of Mint-Up. On the other hand, the asymmetric frame lets the logo appear artistic and innovative.

Logo

Clizia Jewelry

The logo for the Clizia Jewelry was designed by me and Clizia Moradei in October 2016. The minimalistic brand image was shown by a lean sans-serif font and the straight lines of the frame.

Further, the creativity is reflected in the abstract, turquoise shape. This area of tension between the organic and inorganic forms symbolize the philosophy of Clizia Jewelry. Being artistic, but modern.

Poster

Presentation of the font Garamond

For the course Typography and Layout, the task was to show the applicability and character of a font. Adobe Garamond was chosen. From many paper sketches, we created several versions of the poster with Adobe Illustrator.

The large letters in the background give the poster more depth. An artistic look was achieved through the asymmetric positioning of the elements. , the golden cut was considered to attain rhythm.

View the poster

Flyer

Composition and layout

Another exercise for the course Typography and Layout was to design a flyer. The content should have a good composition and typography. Several paper prototypes were created. From them, the best one was digitalized with Adobe Indesign.

In order to reflect the topic beauty matters, an elegant serif font Didot was used. Further, the clean layout with straight shapes gives the flyer a beautiful, but also minimalistic look
