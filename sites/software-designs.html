StagePirate

Presentation improvement software

In November 2017, I designed a single page web design for the startup StagePirate. The founders want to help people become better speakers. The website is currently under construction. Therefore, the software analyzes speeches and gives suggestions for improvement.

The clean and bright design was build with Sketch. The goal was to give it a minimalistic look and make the pages easily accessible. Further, the presentation score should motivate people to improve themselves.

Clizia Jewelry

Jewelry online shop

The goal was to showcase the contemporary minimalistic jewelry of Clizia Moradei. Therefore, a complex online shop was created based on WordPress in October 2016.

The beautiful large pictures and the carefully placed forms give the design an artistic and modern look. The brand values are also reflected in the playful Old Standard TT in contrast with the simple Josefin Sans font.

Mint-Up

Agency website

In May 2017, I designed and developed a single page web design for the agency Mint-Up. Me and David Svilokos wanted to bring great ideas into the web. Our goal was to provide clients with an aesthetic design.

But even more importantly, with designs that are special - having a hint of MINT. To represent this vision, the website has a modern look and an straightforward structure. The website was developed using the Bootstrap component library.
